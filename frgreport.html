Calendar:

May 5, 2005: Marius Mitrea, Colloquium talk at the University of Virginia.

May 19, 2005: Marius Mitrea, One hour plenary address at the 7-th International Conference on Clifford Algebras and Their Applications Toulouse, France.
http://www.icca7.ups-tlse.fr/indexusa.php

May 19, 2005: Dorina Mitrea, Lecture at the 7-th International Conference on Clifford Algebras and Their Applications Toulouse, France.
http://www.icca7.ups-tlse.fr/indexusa.php

June15-July 23, 2005: Alex Iosevich (University of  Missouri) visited the University of Bristol for the purpose of research collaboration with Michael Rudnev.  The visit resulted in the submission of two joint papers.

June 23-July 2, 2005: Alex Iosevich (University of Missouri) visited Philippe Jaming at the University of  Orleans and gave a talk. The visit resulted in a joined project which should result in a paper in the next few months.

July 3-10, 2005: Alex Iosevich (University of Missouri) attended a harmonic analysis meeting at Oberwalfach organized by Detlef Muller and Elias Stein and gave a talk.

July 11-15, 2005: Alex Iosevich (University of Missouri) visited the University of Crete for the purpose of research of collaboration with Mihalis Kolountzakis and gave a talk.

August 1-5, 2005: A week-long program for high school students on the Erdos distance problem at the University of Missouri. In the evenings, an accelerated version of this program was run for undergraduate and graduate students at the University of Missouri-Columbia. Julia Garibaldi (Georgia Institute of Technology) and Alex Iosevich (University of Missouri) ran the program with the assistance of Tyler Jones-Salisbury, a local high school student.

August 29, September 1, September 8, 2005: Michael Rudnev, a series of lectures on distance sets and related problems. See http://www.math.missouri.edu/~dorina for a more detailed description.

December 9-10,2005: Marius Mitrea, speaker at the Seminar on Analysis - On the occasion of Elias Saab's 60th birthday, University of Missouri.

October 2005: A. Iosevich, invited lecture at the Integers Conference at the University of Western Georgia.

October 2005: A. Iosevich, seminar talk at the University of South Carolina.

October 22, 2005: Dorina Mitrea, Special Session on Analysis of Partial Differential Equations, AMS Sectional meeting, Lincoln, NE.

November 2005: Alex Iosevich, colloquium lecture at Kent State University.
