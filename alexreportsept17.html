Ian and I met for about an hour today, September 17, to discuss some ideas for recruiting mathematics majors and minors during their first two years at Mizzou. We agreed that I should write a draft summarizing our discussion and than pass it on to the rest of the group. A rought description of the ideas is the following:

(1) Formation of the committee responsible for collecting information about highly performing first and second year students and channeling them the mathematics club and/or appropriate mentors.

(2) Institution of Math 2980, a lower division version of Math 4980, designed to bring together and enhance the material learned in Calculus I, II and III in a problem solving format.
