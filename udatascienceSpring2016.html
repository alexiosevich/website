Undergraduate Data Science Seminar

The purpose of the seminar is to learn the basics of data science using the python programming language. During the second half of the semester we are going to design research projects related to the material under discussion. The seminar is going to meet on Wednesday evenings at 7.30 p.m. at a location to be determined. Undergraduate participants are encouraged to sign up for Math 395 in conjunction with participating in this seminar.
