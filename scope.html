Scope of Journal

OJAC will publish papers on a wide range of topics, from analysis to number theory and combinatorics, with emphasis on the convergence and interactions between these fields.  We particularly encourage submission of articles which may have one of the following features:

  1. Combinatorial results and analytic methods.
  2. Analytic results and combinatorial methods.
  3. A mixture of combinatorics and analysis in the methods or in their applications.

We also encourage submission of short letters (1-3 pages) describing open research problems of current interest that fall within the scope of the journal. The first 1-2 pages should contain a good description of the subject matter, and the rest should articulate a clearly posed question to be resolved.
