The key to success in this course

The most important key to success in this course is to learn and internalize each proof and example within 24-48 hours of its presentation.
The course will proceed at a rapid pace and attempts to delay the absorption of details until the next exam, or even the next weekend are
likely to result in intestinal discomfort for everybody involved.
