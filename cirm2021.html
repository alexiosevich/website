CIRM (Luminy, France) meeting on Finite Point Configurations

January 4-8, 2021


The purpose of this conference is to bring together senior and junior researchers working on continuous and discrete aspects of finite point configurations from analytic, discrete and ergodic points of view. The problems under consideration include Szemeredi's theorem on arithmetic progressions, Ziegler's result on point configurations inside sets of positive Lebesgue density, as well as recent progress on the Erdos and Falconer distance conjectures. The workshop will be conducted in a friendly and leisurely atmosphere, with no more than five lectures each day and plenty of time for informal discussion and collaboration.
