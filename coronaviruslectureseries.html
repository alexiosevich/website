CoronaVirus Lecture Series

Organizer: Alex Iosevich


The virus is roaming the earth like the Grim Reaper, but mathematics goes on. In the coming days and weeks I am going to post a series of math videos for the University of Rochester community and beyond. These are going to range from undergraduate "Basic Skills" topics to research level material. All the lectures will be designed to be accessible to the widest possible audience.

My current plan is to give the lectures live by Zoom once a week and then post the links to the lectures on this web page.

Please write to me at iosevich@gmail.com with comments and suggestions!

Please stay healthy and safe!

The .pdf files of the beamer presentations that will be used to make the corresponding videos can be obtained by clicking on talk postings below.
