International conference on topics in microlocal analysis, harmonic analysis, and inverse problems



August 15-17, 2022


This conference is dedicated to celebrating the work of Allan Greenleaf who has made important contributions micro-local analysis, harmonic analysis, inverse problems and other areas of mathematics.
