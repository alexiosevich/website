This workshop is being organized by the New York Number Theory Seminar,
and is supported in part by a grant from the Number Theory Foundation.  In
addition to invited lectures there will be a session for contributed
papers.  Mathematicians, and especially graduate students, are
encouraged to attend.  We intend that the lectures will be sufficiently
expository that the number theorists will learn from the harmonic
analysts, and conversely.  For further information, or to be added to the
email list for this workshop, please contact Mel Nathanson at
nathansn@alpha.lehman.cuny.edu or by phone at (718) 960-8860.
